Education support

Parentless Student Education Support

Educational support is provided for parentless students and poor students from school level through college level. The program includes school bags, uniforms, notebooks, learning materials, fee assistance, and continuity guidance so students can remain in education without interruption.

Student development

Sports Materials Support

Sports material support helps students and youth participate in physical activity, school events, team games, and confidence-building programs. The trust supports sports kits, uniforms, footballs, shoes, and event-day encouragement for young players.

Shelter support

Homes for Homeless Families

Housing support for families without safe shelter, including temporary shelter materials, roof protection, basic construction help, local coordination, and essential household setup support.

Livelihood support

Self-Employment Support for Poor Families

Livelihood support for poor families through tailoring machines, women-focused training, tutorials, and trade tools for home-based income.

Medical support

Support for People Affected by Leprosy

Medical follow-up, food and nutrition support, essential supplies, mobility assistance, and dignity-focused outreach for people affected by leprosy.

Accessibility support

Blind People Support

Food kits, daily living support, mobility assistance, and community inclusion help for visually impaired people and families.

Food support

Food Giveaway for Roadside and Needy People

Cooked meals, drinking water, and basic food support are given directly to roadside people, children, elderly people, and needy families through village visits and highway outreach.

Community welfare

Irular ST People Support

Support for Irular Scheduled Tribe families through direct village outreach, essential mats and bedsheets, nutrition items, family welfare visits, and community inclusion assistance.

Women empowerment

Widow and Women Support

Support for widow and needy women through rice bags, grocery help, household essentials, children care support, and practical guidance for stable family living.

Children welfare

Children Welfare

Children welfare support includes meals, groceries, clothing, slippers, safe transport help, and emergency care for vulnerable children and families.
